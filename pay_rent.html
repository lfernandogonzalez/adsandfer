Paying Rent

Rent is due the first of every month. You can pay rent using one of the following methods:

Direct Withdrawal

The most convenient way to pay rent is to set up direct withdrawal with SingleKey. Rent will automatically be withdrawn from your bank account on the first of every month. You will be reminded via email ahead of the withdrawal.

Email Transfer

Rent can be paid with an email transfer to adsandferproperties@gmail.com. Please include your address, unit number and month(s) being paid in the comment section.

Cheque

Cheques can be made to Adsandfer Properties and mailed to 58 Saginaw Cres, Ottawa, ON. K2E-5N7. Please include your address, unit number and month(s) being paid in the memo section. We ask that you provide post-dated cheques for six months at the time.
