Welcome!

Thank you for renting from Adsandfer Properties. This handbook is designed to help you settle in, understand your rights and responsibilities, and get the most out of living in our property. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Moving In

  • Keys: Each tenant receives keys to their unit and any shared areas. Lost keys will incur a replacement fee of $20.
  • Utility Setup: Water and Gas are included in all rentals. Unless otherwise specified in your rental agreement, each tenant is responsible for setting up their own Hydro and Internet account.
  • Insurance: Tenants are encouraged to have renter's insurance for personal belongings.

Rent Payments

  • Due Date: Rent is due on the first day of each month.
  • Payment Methods: You can pay via cheque, email transfer, or direct withdrawal. Visit our Pay Rent section on our website for details.
  • Late Fees: Late or declined payments will incur a fee of $20.

Maintenance and Repairs

  • Routine Maintenance: Notify the landlord immediately of any issues (e.g., plumbing, heating, or appliance problems) by submitting a Maintenance Request form.
  • Emergency Repairs: For urgent issues (like water leaks or heating failures), call us directly at the numbers above.
  • Tenant Responsibility: Tenants are responsible for minor maintenance (e.g., changing lightbulbs). If damage occurs due to tenant neglect, repair costs may be the tenant’s responsibility.

Garbage, Recycling, and Compost

  • Garbage Pickup: Details on garbage collection schedules will be provided during your orientation.
  • Recycling and Compost: Recycling bins are located near the entrance. Please follow local guidelines for disposal.

Shared Spaces

  • Hallways and Common Areas: Hallways and entryways must be kept clear of personal items for safety.
  • Outdoor Spaces: Tenants may share outdoor areas, such as a yard or patio. Respect others’ use of these spaces and maintain cleanliness.

Parking

  • Parking Spots: Parking is reserved for tenants. Visitor parking is only permitted on spots marked for visitors, if any.
  • Snow Removal: Tenants are responsible for clearing their parking spots in winter.

Quiet Hours and Noise

To maintain a peaceful environment, quiet hours are from 9 PM to 7 AM. Please be mindful of noise levels, especially in shared spaces, outdoors, or when hosting guests.

Pets

Owners are fully responsible for any property damage caused by their pets. All pets must be safe for other tenants, well-behaved, and non-aggressive. Any reports of aggressive behavior or repeated disturbances may require the pet’s removal from the property.

Lease Terms

  • Tenancy Term: Unless otherwise stated in your lease agreement, the tenancy term is one year, followed by a month-to-month arrangement.
  • End of Lease: Tenants are required to provide a minimum of 60 days' notice before terminating the lease. During this period, tenants must allow showings of the unit to prospective renters and maintain the apartment in a clean and tidy condition for this purpose.
  • Moving Out: Leave the apartment in good condition, remove all belongings, and return keys.

Health and Safety

  • Fire Alarms and Smoke Detectors: Tampering with fire alarms or smoke detectors is strictly prohibited. Notify the landlord if any alarms are malfunctioning.
  • Safety Checks: Occasionally, the landlord may perform safety checks with prior notice.

Tenant Responsibilities and Code of Conduct

  • Respect for Neighbors: Be courteous to other tenants, respect common areas, and communicate openly.
  • Smoking: Smoking is prohibited indoors and in common areas. Outdoor smoking must occur at least 9 meters (30 feet) away from building entrances and exits, in compliance with the Smoke-Free Ontario Act.
  • Illegal Activity: Illegal activity is grounds for immediate termination of the lease.

Communication and Updates

We will provide updates through email, posted notices, and on our website. Please ensure we have your current contact information on file.

Thank You!

Thank you for being part of our community. We hope you enjoy your time here. Please reach out if you have any questions or concerns.
